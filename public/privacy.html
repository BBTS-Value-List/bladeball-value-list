Who controls this data

BBTSL is the controller for personal data processed through bbtsl.lol. You can contact the project team at bbtsl@pve.bio. BBTSL does not currently appoint a data protection officer.

This notice is written to provide the information required by GDPR Articles 12 to 14. The European Commission explains the required information and your rights in its Article 13 guidance and the binding text is available in GDPR Article 13.

BBTSL does not use advertising cookies, behavioural advertising, analytics pixels, or automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

Discord sign-in and the public API

Discord sign-in is optional. When you select it, Discord authenticates you and returns the identity fields listed above. Discord processes its own services under its Privacy Policy.

The public, read-only BBTSL API does not require a Discord bot token, Discord message content, guild membership, or a Discord user ID. The website does not receive those fields from a public API request. This policy covers the website and public API only.

Sharing and international transfers

We use Cloudflare to host, secure, and deliver the website and its database. Cloudflare acts as a processor for customer content and request data where applicable. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for its processing information.

Website fonts are served from BBTSL. Opening a page does not request fonts from a third-party font provider.

Discord receives information needed for its OAuth sign-in flow. Cloudflare, Google, and Discord may process data outside your country, including outside the EEA or UK. Their own notices describe the safeguards and transfer arrangements they use. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

  • Session cookies last up to 12 hours. The short OAuth state cookie lasts up to 10 minutes.
  • Rate-limit records are short-lived and are pruned automatically.
  • Discord profile and role records remain while the account is needed for access control or public team attribution. Disabled accounts are not shown publicly.
  • Staff audit records remain while needed to protect the integrity of value-list edits and recover from mistakes.
  • Public item and media records remain until changed or removed by authorised staff.

We review retention when a record is no longer needed. If you request erasure, we will assess the request against our security and audit obligations.

Your GDPR rights

Subject to the conditions in applicable law, you can ask for access, correction, erasure, restriction, portability, or object to processing based on legitimate interests. You can also complain to the data protection authority where you live, work, or believe an infringement occurred. The European Commission lists these rights in its GDPR rights guidance.

Email bbtsl@pve.bio with “Privacy Request” in the subject line. Tell us which Discord account or email address to use to locate the record. We may ask for proportionate proof of identity before disclosing or changing account data. We aim to respond within one month, as described in GDPR Article 12.

Changes

We will update this policy when the website, public API, or legal requirements materially change. The effective date at the top shows the latest version.

BBTSL is an unofficial community project and is not affiliated with Roblox, Blade Ball, or their owners.
